Proactive Operations Management

Keep your enterprise systems up-to-date and protected from digital disruption. Intrex Digital Hub acts as your complete IT operations partner, providing round-the-clock monitoring and remote support for your servers, systems, and network endpoints. We identify and resolve errors before they can disrupt employee productivity.

Key Features & Capabilities

  • 24/7 Desktop Remote Helpdesk: Swift remote troubleshooting and diagnostics for user workstations, emails, printers, and productivity tools.
  • Device & Network Health Monitoring: Active link monitoring and hardware diagnostics to locate and report potential failure points early.
  • Patch Management & OS Updates: Secure, automated update deployments for Windows/Linux systems and databases, keeping vulnerabilities sealed.
  • System Security Scans: Daily malware detection, firewall log analyses, and database backup verification routines.

Business Value

Outsourcing your system administration requirements to our team eliminates the high cost of maintaining full-time internal IT departments. You secure custom SLA guarantees that hold us accountable for systems uptime, allowing your management to focus fully on scaling core products.
